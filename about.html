About Dr. Ranjit Rajak

Dr. Ranjit Rajak received his MCA, M.Tech., and Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology from JNU, New Delhi, India in the years 2007, 2009, and 2014,He completed his B.C.A (Bachelor of Computer Applications) from CCS Meerut University, Meerut, U.P., India His area of interest includes Parallel Computing, Scheduling Problem, WSN, and Cloud Computing. He has been working as an Assistant Professor since May 2013 in the Department of Computer Science and Applications, Dr. Harisingh Gour Central University, Sagar (M.P), and has published over 60 papers in journals, conferences, and book chapters in national and international repute. He is a reviewer of Wireless Personal Communication (Springer) journal, Cluster Computing (Springer) journal, and many more reputed journals. He is also a member of IAENG and CSI.
